Types Sorting Algorithms

There are two broad types of sorting algorithms

1. Quadratic

  • Quadratic
    The complexity is proportional to the square of n.
    An example of a quadratic sorting algorithm is Bubble sort, with a time complexity of O(n2).



    • 2. Logarithmic


  • Logarithmic
    The complexity is proportional to the binary logarithm (i.e to the base 2) of n.
    An example of a logarithmic sorting algorithm is Quick sort, with space and time complexity O(n Ã— log n).
